### No.1 — 你的镜片和普通镜片长得不一样！

**核心信息**
- 中央圆形区：清晰区，看远清楚
- 周边功能区：一圈一圈错落的小纹路 = "秘密武器"，专门保护眼睛

**情景解读**
让孩子理解自己眼镜的特殊性，把「不一样」转化为「厉害」，建立信心而非陌生感。

**插画要点**

- 中央区：明亮光芒，光线汇聚效果
- 周边区：同心圆纹路清晰可见，像城堡雉堞环形纹

**生图提示词（English）**
```
An educational diagram illustration of a round eyeglass lens viewed from the front, 
designed like a heroic shield emblem. The center circle glows brightly with converging 
light rays symbolizing clear central vision. The surrounding concentric rings have 
beautiful regularly-spaced rectangular notch patterns (like castle battlements arranged 
in circles), representing the "secret weapon" peripheral myopia-control zone. A cute 
cartoon Asian boy character peeks from the bottom corner, holding a magnifying glass 
and pointing excitedly at the ring patterns with wide eyes and a "wow" expression. 
Blue gradient background behind the lens. White sparkles and glow effects on the lens 
rim. Simple label arrows (no text visible). Educational infographic composition.
```

### No.2 — 刚开始的小变化，都是正常的！

**核心信息**
- 大多数小朋友戴上就适应，少数需要约1周
- 周边偶尔模糊、有点晃 = 正常，眼睛在识别适应
- 要有信心，坚持戴，慢慢习惯

**情景解读**
用卡通化方式将"不适感"可视化，让孩子和家长看到后觉得"原来这很正常、甚至有点可爱"，消除焦虑。

**视觉设计关键**
- "适应中"文字设计成倾斜/摇晃感（后期排版）
- Day 2 标记
- 眼睛漩涡 + 星星 = 轻度晕眩的卡通表达

**插画要点**
- 戴眼镜女孩，表情轻微困惑但不惊慌（参考image5.png的漩涡眼表达）
- 头顶飘星星 + 动态曲线
- 进度条感的背景装饰
- 旁边可以有家长/朋友举拳头鼓励的小元素

**生图提示词（English）**
```
A cute Asian girl aged 10 wearing round glasses, sitting on a chair with a mildly 
dizzy but calm expression — her eyes show cartoon spiral/swirly patterns (like the 
classic cartoon dizzy effect) indicating the adapting sensation. Around her head float 
white four-pointed stars and gentle wavy motion lines creating a soft dizziness aura. 
The overall body language is relaxed, not panicked. Small visual elements suggest 
"Day 2" (like a tiny calendar badge). Background has gentle tilted lines and soft 
circular motion graphics to reinforce the "adapting" feeling. To the side, a tiny 
fist-bump encouragement icon or cheerful expression from a parent. The mood is 
humorous and reassuring — this is normal and temporary. Warm blue-purple background.
```

### No.4 — 第二步：学看远（双联对比图）

**核心信息**
1. 抬头平视前方，用中央区看远 → 非常清楚
2. 转动眼睛用周边区看远 → 模糊晃动没关系

**情景解读**
这张卡片用「前后对比」的方式，让孩子主动体验两种视觉的差异，建立对周边区模糊的接受度。

**插画要点**
- 左右两图，中间竖虚线分隔
- 场景统一：男孩站教室窗前，窗外红旗+标语
- 左图：眼球正视，聚焦线，背景清晰
- 右图：眼球侧转，同心波纹模糊效果，思维气泡"模糊一点没关系！"

**生图提示词（English）**
```
A split-panel educational illustration divided by a dashed vertical line in the center. 
LEFT PANEL — "Central Zone, Clear Vision": An Asian boy aged 10 stands confidently 
by a bright classroom window, head up, looking straight ahead. Through the window: 
a flagpole with a red Chinese flag and a banner sign. Three parallel straight lines 
(focus beams) beam from his glasses center outward to the distant objects. Window 
scene is sharp and vivid. Boy's expression: focused and confident. RIGHT PANEL — 
"Peripheral Zone, Slightly Blurry": Exact same boy, same pose, same window scene, 
but his eyeballs are visibly rotated to the side (showing whites more). The flagpole 
and sign are now soft and blurry with concentric ripple rings radiating from them. 
A friendly thought bubble floats beside his head with "模糊没关系" meaning. His 
expression is relaxed and accepting. Composition: two equal panels, same background 
environment for easy comparison.
```

## 三、生图参数建议

| 参数 | 推荐值 | 原因 |
|------|--------|------|
| 宽高比 | 9:16 | 卡片80×130mm为标准竖版 |
| 画面留白 | 上下各留约15-20% | 为标题/正文排版预留 |
| 人物数量 | 1-2人为主 | 避免画面拥挤 |
| 画面内文字 | 无 | 全部后期在设计软件中添加 |
| 风格锁定 | 每次携带Style DNA | 保持跨卡片风格一致 |
| 背景复杂度 | 简化 | 背景服务于人物，不抢主体 |

## 四、风格锁定工作流程建议

```
阶段1 - 风格探索：
  编辑 Style DNA → 生成测试图 → 调整 → 再生成 → 直到满意

阶段2 - 风格锁定：
  点击「锁定风格」→ Style DNA 固定 → 后续所有卡片自动携带

阶段3 - 批量生产：
  选卡片 → 微调场景描述 → 生成 → 保存 → 下一张

关键原则：
  完整提示词 = [Style DNA] + [场景描述] + [构图提示]
  Style DNA 锁定后不变，场景描述每张不同
```

## 五、提示词优化技巧

### 增强风格一致性的关键词组
- `consistent character design throughout` — 人物设计统一
- `same art style as previous illustrations` — 延续风格
- `flat color fills, no gradients except subtle` — 扁平色块
- `child-appropriate, non-threatening imagery` — 儿童友好

### 增强画面质量的关键词组
- `clean composition, clear focal point` — 构图清晰
- `balanced negative space` — 留白平衡
- `readable at small sizes` — 小尺寸可读
- `high contrast between character and background` — 对比度好

### 需要避免的关键词
- `realistic`, `photorealistic` — 会破坏卡通风格
- `complex background` — 背景抢主体
- `many characters` — 人物过多拥挤
- `dark colors` — 保持明亮积极基调
